Gemstone Appraisal

Our professional gemstone appraisal service provides accurate market valuations for insurance, estate planning, resale, or personal knowledge. Our certified appraisers follow industry standards and use current market data to ensure your gemstones are properly valued.

Types of Appraisals We Provide

Insurance Appraisal

Replacement value appraisals for insurance coverage, ensuring your gemstones are adequately protected against loss, theft, or damage.

Estate Appraisal

Fair market value assessments for estate planning, inheritance distribution, or tax purposes, following IRS guidelines and legal requirements.

Resale Appraisal

Current market value evaluations for selling purposes, providing realistic expectations for resale transactions in today's market.

Personal Knowledge

Comprehensive evaluations for personal understanding of your collection's value, quality, and significance in the gemstone market.

Our Appraisal Process

1

Initial Consultation

We discuss your appraisal needs, intended use, and timeline to ensure we provide the most appropriate service for your requirements.

2

Gemstone Examination

Thorough analysis of each gemstone including identification, quality grading, measurements, and documentation of all relevant characteristics.

3

Market Research

Current market analysis using industry databases, auction results, and dealer networks to establish accurate pricing for similar gemstones.

4

Value Determination

Professional valuation based on gemstone quality, rarity, market demand, and the specific purpose of the appraisal.

5

Report Preparation

Comprehensive written appraisal report with detailed descriptions, photographs, and professional opinions meeting industry standards.
