About Mineral Gemstones

Hello, I’m Rafael Ferreira, founder of Mineral Gemstones. After years on a conventional career path, I chose to build a life around what truly inspires me: gemstones and the natural beauty of stone.

I’m an advanced student at GIA (Gemological Institute of America), committed to rigorous standards for gemstone identification and valuation. That same technical mindset guides my work with natural stones for interiors.

What I do: I act as your consultant and curator. I help you choose the right material (semi-precious slabs, quartzites, marbles, backlit onyx), introduce trusted partner vendors and support pricing negotiations — so you get beauty, performance and value.

Important: Mineral Gemstones provides consulting only. Measurements, drawings, fabrication, logistics and installation are handled by third-party professionals.
