About Mineral Gemstones

Hello, my name is Rafael Ferreira, and I am the founder of Mineral Gemstones. For many years, I followed a conventional professional path, but I always felt something was missing: a true connection with my work. I found that connection in the fascinating and eternal world of gemstones. Living in Los Angeles for over ten years, I decided to transform a long-standing passion into a profession. I immersed myself in studies and today I am a proud advanced student of the Gemological Institute of America (GIA), the largest and most respected global authority in gemology, pursuing the prestigious Graduate Gemologist training. Mineral Gemstones was born from my personal journey and the desire to bring clarity, confidence, and transparency to the jewelry market. My goal is not just to appraise a stone; it is to understand its history, its journey from the mine to the jewel, and to translate its value honestly and accurately for you. Whether you are a collector, someone who inherited a family jewel, or are about to make one of the most important purchases of your life, I am here to be your trusted guide. Combining the technical rigor learned at GIA with a genuine passion, my commitment is to ensure you understand and appreciate the true value of what you hold in your hands.
