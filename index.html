Stones for Jewelry

This service is focused on natural colored stones intended for finished jewelry or custom jewelry projects. Whether the client needs a center stone for a ring, a matched pair for earrings, side stones for a design, or a stone for a pendant, we help identify the best options based on color, shape, cut, size, quality and overall visual impact.

The goal is not simply to sell a stone, but to help the client choose a stone that makes sense aesthetically and commercially, creating stronger results for jewelry design, gifting or resale.

Custom Sourcing

Custom sourcing is designed for clients who need something specific and do not want to waste time searching blindly. This includes requests for exact colors, calibrated sizes, particular cuts, matched pairs, rare varieties, or stones selected for a defined budget range.

This process adds value because it combines sourcing knowledge, supplier access and selection criteria to bring clients options that are more aligned with their needs, instead of random inventory that does not fit the project.

Trade & Private Clients

We work with both business and individual buyers. Trade clients may include jewelry designers, retailers, resellers or industry professionals looking for reliable stone selection and sourcing support. Private clients may be collectors, gift buyers or individuals searching for a special stone with confidence and guidance.

This dual positioning allows Mineral Gemstones to serve different levels of demand while maintaining a professional, transparent and results-oriented approach.
