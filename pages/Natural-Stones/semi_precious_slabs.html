Semi-Precious Slabs

Statement slabs in Allure Agate, Blue Agate, Quartz and Petrified Wood. We curate, design the layout (bookmatch/quadmatch), and deliver ready for fabrication—backlit options available.

What We Deliver

  • Curated selection: Allure/Blue Agate, premium Quartz, Petrified Wood.
  • Layout planning: vein-match, bookmatch, quadmatch.
  • Backlighting design: LED spec, diffusers, power planning.
  • Inspection, crating and insured transport to your fabricator.

Process

  1. Brief & measurements.
  2. Remote or in-person slab selection.
  3. Layout approval & cutting plan.
  4. Crate, ship, and coordinate with fabricator.

Specs & Options

  • Thickness: 2cm / 3cm; panels 10–20mm.
  • Backlight: 3000–5000K, CRI ≥90, dimmable drivers.
  • Finishes: polished, honed/brushed, resin-treated.

FAQs

Can I use slabs in kitchens? Yes—best for islands and feature panels. For heavy-use counters, we specify sealers and heat protection.

Quick Info
