Backlit Onyx Walls

Dramatic, translucent onyx walls engineered with proper lighting: color temperature, CRI, diffusion and power access—specified for even glow without hotspots.

What We Deliver

  • Lighting design: target lux, uniformity and maintainability.
  • Diffusers (PMMA/Glass), standoff depths and ventilation.
  • Power routing, drivers and dimming strategy.

Process

  1. Concept & light study (3000–4000K, CRI ≥90).
  2. Mock-up of lightbox and diffuser spacing.
  3. Fabrication drawings and vendor coordination.
  4. Commissioning and handover documentation.

Specs & Options

  • LED panels/strips, 24V drivers, PWM/0–10V dimming.
  • Access panels for service, thermal management.
  • Finish: polished/honed onyx, resin-back options.

FAQs

Will I see hotspots? We calculate diffuser distance and light density to avoid hotspots and banding.
