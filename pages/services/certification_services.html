Certification Services

Our certification services provide official documentation and authentication of gemstone authenticity and quality. We issue professional certificates that are recognized internationally and meet the highest industry standards. Our certifications are essential for insurance purposes, resale transactions, and establishing the provenance of valuable gemstones.

Types of Certificates We Issue

Gemstone Identification Certificate

This certificate provides definitive identification of the gemstone species and variety, including detailed gemological properties, measurements, and any treatments detected. It serves as official documentation of the stone's identity and natural or enhanced characteristics.

Quality Grading Certificate

Our quality grading certificates evaluate gemstones according to internationally recognized standards, including the famous 4Cs for diamonds (Cut, Color, Clarity, Carat) and equivalent quality factors for colored stones. These certificates provide comprehensive quality assessments that are crucial for determining market value.

Origin Determination Certificate

For premium gemstones where geographic origin significantly affects value, we provide origin determination certificates. Using advanced analytical techniques, we can often determine the likely geographic source of gemstones, which is particularly important for rubies, sapphires, and emeralds from famous localities.

Treatment Disclosure Certificate

This specialized certificate focuses on detecting and documenting any treatments or enhancements applied to gemstones. We use sophisticated analytical equipment to identify heat treatment, irradiation, fracture filling, and other common enhancement methods. Full disclosure of treatments is essential for ethical trade and accurate valuation.

Certification Standards and Accreditation

Our certification services adhere to the strictest international standards and are conducted by GIA-certified gemologists. We follow protocols established by leading gemological institutions worldwide, ensuring our certificates are recognized and accepted by insurance companies, auction houses, and gemstone dealers globally. Our laboratory maintains the highest levels of accuracy and integrity in all certification processes.

Certificate Features and Security

Our certificates incorporate multiple security features to prevent counterfeiting and ensure authenticity. Each certificate includes high-resolution photographs of the gemstone, detailed technical specifications, unique identification numbers, watermarks, and security printing. We also maintain a secure database that allows verification of certificate authenticity online, providing additional protection against fraud.
